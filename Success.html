What is
SunnyMoon Project?

We are a virtual creative art studio specializing in creating art assets for mobile
gaming and animation, as well as character designs and visual development and
overall branding.

Our talented team of artists and designers have meticulous attention to detail and a
deep understanding of the power of visuals. We also believe in building strong
partnerships with our clients to ensure their vision is fully realized.

“ Whether you're a game developer in need of stunning art assets, or a brand
seeking visuals that truly stand out from the rest, Sunnymoon Project is here to
bring your creative vision to life. ”
